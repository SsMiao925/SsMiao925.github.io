Shuangkang Fang (方双康)

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in communication and information systems, at Beihang University, supervised by Professor Wenrui Ding and Reseacher Yufeng Wang. Additionally, I receive academic guidance from Professor Ming-Hsuan Yang.

From 2020 to 2022, I worked full-time at Megvii Inc., supervised by Yi Yang, and Shuchang Zhou . From 2022 to the present, I worked an internship at Megvii Inc. and StepFun Inc., also supervised by Yi Yang, and Shuchang Zhou .

My research interests lie in the scope of computer vision. More specifically, my current focus is on Neural Implicit Representation, 3D Scene Editing, and early works were on Image Caption and Model Quantization.
