Sasa Miao (苗洒洒)

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Zhejiang Academy of Agricultural Sciences. In July 2024, I obtained my Ph.D. in Animal Nutrition and Feed Science from Zhejiang University, under the supervision of Professor Xiaoting Zou and Associate Researcher Xinyang Dong.

My research mainly focuses on the nutritional regulation of fatty liver and intestinal metabolic function by sodium butyrate, as well as the effects of heavy metals on ovarian damage and granulosa cell-cell proliferation and apoptosis.
